Ingredients:
- Neutral Oil (2 cups)
- Garlic (10 cloves)
- Shallot (1 large)
- Gochugru (⅔ cup)
- Chili Flakes (⅔ cup)
- Ginger (2in knob)
- Shitake Mushroom Powder (2 tsp)
- Sugar (2 tbsp)
- MSG (2 tsp)
- Rice Vinegar (2 tbsp)
- Soy Sauce (2 tbsp)

Steps:
1. Mince the garlic and thin slice the Shallot
2. In separate bowl, combine the Gochugru, Chili Flakes, Shitake Mushroom Powder, Sugar, MSG and Ginger(Grated).
3. Add the neutral oil on the pot, after that add the Garlic and Shallot. Heat the pot and turn of after the it golden brown. (Do not heat and add the garlic & shallot).
4. After that drain the garlic and shallot, heat again the oil on the pot and pour it in the spices.
5. Add the soy sauce, rice vinegar and sesami seeds, Once it's cold and the garlic and shallot.
