Ingredients:
Garlic - 4 cloves
Ginger - 1 tps
Onion - 2 pieces
Pork - 1/4 kg
Carrots - 1 pcs
Salt - 1/2 tps
Black pepper - 1/2 tps
Oil - 1 tbps
Soy sauce - 1/4 cup
Merin - 1/4 cup
Oyster sauce - 2 tbps
Liquid Seasoning - 2 tbps
Star anis - 2 pcs
Dried Bayleafs - 1 or 2 pcs
Sugar - 1 tps
Butter - 1 tbps
Bok choy - its up to you
Sesami oil - 1/2 tps
Corn starch slurry

Steps:
 
1. Stir fry the pork and season it with salt and pepper, until it turned golden brow, set aside.
2. Same pan, Saute the Garlic, Ginger and Onion.
3. Put back the pork after the spices get cooked.
4. Add water, put enough to submerge the pork.
5. Now, add the soy sauce, merin, oyster sauce, and liquid seasoning. Lower the heat to medium heat to seamer it.
6. If the pork is soft enough to pierce by fork. Add star anis and dried bay leaf. Add more water if needed.
7. Add Carrots , add the slurry, seamer again in 3 mins. 
8. Finish it with sugar and butter.

Step for Bok choi:
1. Steam it or boil it on water.
2. Glaze it with a mixture of oyster sauce and sesami oil
3. Add friend garlic on top
